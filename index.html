How to play?

Please allow the browser to open camera, this will not record any privacy.

Keep your computer at a distance (for me 1.5 meters) that can see your upper body and skeleton on the screen. And then start the game.

  • Arms up and down to hit 1st or 3rd columns
  • Hands clap up and down to hit the middle column

You can also use mouse click (If you are using mobile devices, pose estimation will be disabled)

We recommend Google Chrome to play this game
